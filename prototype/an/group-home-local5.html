About

Kensington Greenpeace
About:

Greenpeace Kensington is the new name for West London Greenpeace! We meet on the first Tuesday of every month at 7.30pm near Earl's Court station. You don't need to live in Kensington to join us - we have people coming over from various parts of London to our meetings, campaigns and actions. New members are always welcome!
